I am PhD Student in the Epidemiology Unit under the Faculty of Medicine at Prince of Songkla University. I am also trained as a medical doctor and have more than four years of experience in the field of TB and HIV. I also participate as a junior facilitator in research training courses known as "Structured Operational Research Training IniTiative" (SORT-IT). I love Epidemiology and Statistics. My current research focuses on international migrants and role of insurances on the diagnosis and treatment in this population.
