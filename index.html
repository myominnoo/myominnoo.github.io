Hello, everyone!


I am a PhD Student in the Epidemiology Unit under the Faculty of Medicine at Prince of Songkla University. I am also trained as a medical doctor and have more than four years of experience in the field of TB and HIV. Previously I worked for The Union for the past four years. I also involve as a junior facilitator in research training courses known as "Structured Operational Research Training IniTiative" (SORT-IT). I love Epidemiology and Statistics. I also teach EpiData software and sometimes work on data management and analysis using EpiData and R. My current research focuses on international migrants and role of insurances on the diagnosis and treatment in this population.
