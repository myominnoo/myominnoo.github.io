EpiData

As part of my job, I facilitate in research training courses including Structured Operational Research Training Initiative (SORT-IT) courses. One of the major focuses of these courses is on data collection which is often neglected. The skills set are groomed using the EpiData software suite and applying the concept of good clinical practice, double data entry and validation. The aim is to achieve good data quality for the research credibility.

R and RStudio

When it comes to data management and manipulation, I love R as a personal preference and of course, RStudio along with it. Everyone says R has a deep learning curve. Amen to that. But I must say it's really worth learning it. RStudio has a nice GUI and a tons of features with it including rmarkdown and notebook. The core R itself is a functional software for many data and statistical processes. Here I noted down what I learned to help you have a less steep learning curve for R.
