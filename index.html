Hi, everyone!


I am Myo. I am a medical doctor working in the area of TB and HIV for 5+ years. Since 2014, I worked at The Union in several positions for four years, as research fellow for the last two years. I was also an organizer and facilitator in research training courses known as "Structured Operational Research Training IniTiative" (SORT-IT).

As a hobby, I love reading books. One of my favorites is "The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho. I also love solving challenges, thus statistics, computer science and epidemiology. I teach how to use softwares and work on data management and analysis using EpiData and R. View my channel on Youtube for more tutorial videos.

Currenlty I am doing PhD in Epidemiology and live in Thailand. My current research project is to study the effect of health insurance status on delays in healthcare and treatment outcomes among migrant population in Thailand.
