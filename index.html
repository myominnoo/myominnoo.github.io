Hi! I am Myo Minn Oo, a public health researcher and PhD candidate at the Epidemiology Unit, Prince of Songkla University in Thailand. I conduct health-related researches with an aim to inform better policies and practices to health systems. My ambition is to change the world around me through evidence-based actions. I also love to learn data science, often make videos and build analytic tools. for 5+ years. I am from Myanmar but currently live in Mae Sot where I do my PhD research project.

Current Works

Primary focus is my PhD research project. It is about situational assessment of health insurance, delays into healthcare, treatment outcomes among among Myanmar migrants who fall ill with tuberculosis, and changes in quality of life, stigma and social support while they are on treatment. The project period is one and a half year and now in the phase of data collection.


Outside work, I am writing a guidebook for EpiData Manager and EntryClient. I am also developing two R packages:

  • mStats for my own use which is based on ggplot2 for plotting, and
  • stats2 - an upgraded version from mStats that contains functions for easy and quick use.
