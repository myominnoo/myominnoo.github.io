Resume

A short biography

Dr. Myo Min Oo is a PhD candidate in Epidemiology at the Faculty of Medicine, Prince of Songkla University of Thailand. After earning his medical degree, Dr. Myo joined The Union Office in Myanmar and worked in TB clinical and research works. He last worked as research fellow before joining his PhD and were involved in several research training courses known as SORT IT. He is one of research team for this PhD thesis project on TB and migrants. The project receives funding from Health System Research Institute, Thailand and a sub-grant of National Institute of Health, United States.
